Dragon-changer

  • High Concept: Two bunnies fight to capture each other's revered golden apples.
  • Player Experience Goals: A challenging and competitive experience that should frustrate, excite and motivate the player to score as high as they can.
  • Genre: Platformer/Competitive Capture
  • Game play: The player controls the white bunny with the arrow keys and must travel to the enemy's base to pick up their golden apple and bring it back to their own camp to score. While the player has the apple, if they are touched by an angry bunny, they lose the apple and must start over. All the while, the blue bunny is trying to capture the player's apple. The player can reset the blue bunny by touching him, but if the blue bunny captures the player apple, the player loses.
  • Prototype goal: Integrating competitive play and a capture objective. Also, giving enemies set paths using collision boxes and changng velocities.
  • Features: This capture game gives the player several things to thing about and manage while trying to score as high as they can. It features an enemy player who acts on their own and attempts to beat you. The Golden Apples also has several moving enemies to make the objective more difficult.
  • Setting/Premise: A forest full of life and inhabited by bunnies (mostly angry) which also houses mystical and sparkling golden apples.
  • Story: The hero bunny seeks to gain glory for their own bunny clan by capturing the rival bunny clan's golden apples. All the while, the rival bunny clan is working against the hero to both stop them and capture the hero's golden apple.
  • Target Audience: Young adults/children.
  • Play time: 4-5 minutes.
  • Strategies: It's important to look at the map before setting off and to keep an eye on the blue bunny while running back and forth to capture the enemy's golden apple.
